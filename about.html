About

When coming up with the name for my company, I wanted something that would represent my company well, Higher Tide Media did just that. Just as it’s stated in the name “Higher Tide,” my company strives at producing top notch media, a higher standard of design and experiences. My goal was to have a company that not only could serve the “big dogs” in the media world, but also cared enough about the smaller ones as well. At HigherTideMedia, not only do we do just that, we also provide the best all around experience for the client.

Whether it’s the brand new sports store on the corner of 5th and Main, the stay-at-home mom who sells holiday wreaths on the side, your brothers new rock band or the local church event, Higher Tide Media is the right company for the job. Higher Tide Media appeals to all ages, occupations and backgrounds. If it’s professional, high quality standards and a great client experience they are looking for, Higher Tide Media is the right choice hands down.

Shawn Lawson
Higher Tide Media Owner
